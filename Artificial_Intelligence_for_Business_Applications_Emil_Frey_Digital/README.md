**Course Objective:** The goal of this course is to introduce students to the practical application of artificial intelligence methods in business contexts, with emphasis on data-driven decision making, predictive modelling, and the development of intelligent systems that solve real business problems.

**About the Project: Digital Vehicle Sales Optimization** This project was developed in collaboration with Emil Frey Digital as part of an academic-industry partnership. The goal was to identify the most profitable online advertising platform for vehicle sales using a Decision Support System (DSS) built on real business data. The project covers the full data science pipeline across four integrated datasets (900,000+ records): data cleaning, feature engineering, and integration, followed by training and optimization of Random Forest and XGBoost classifiers for contract success prediction. Platform selection was further validated using multi-criteria Analytic Hierarchy Process (AHP) analysis across five business criteria, cross-referenced with model sensitivity analysis. The final recommendation provides segment-specific advertising strategies per vehicle type and price range.
